H2 - Dense cirrus with puffs and wispy edges
H3 - Dense anvil shaped cirrus, originally the remains of a CB cloud
H4 - Cirrus increasing

H6 - Increasing cirrostratus not covering the sky
H7 - Cirrostratus covering the entire sky
H8 - Veil of cirrostratus not covering the sky

M5 - Multiple levels of altocumulus
M6 - Altocumulus formed by spreading cumulus
M7 Altocumulus with altostratus
M8 Altocumulus castellanus
M9 Altocumulus causing a chaotic sky

L2 - Moderate cumulus
